About Us

School of Hospitality and Tourism is the centre of excellence in hospitality education and training, through courses that are designed for graduates who aim to develop their career by gaining applied knowledge in the spheres of Hospitality and Tourism. SCHOTO is a well known brand which boasts of fully equipped state of the art training facilities, located in Bulawayo.

It has emerged as the hub for human capital development in hospitality education and training.The School of Hospitality and Tourism is committed to shaping industry-ready professionals with strong values, leadership, and hands-on experience. Our programs blend practical training with academic excellence to prepare you for success in hotels, restaurants, travel, and tourism.

  • Experienced faculty and industry mentors
  • Modern training facilities and labs
  • Internships and real-world exposure
